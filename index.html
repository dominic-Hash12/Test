About the Project

This Virtual Campus Tour provides students and visitors with an immersive way to explore St. Joseph's University using first-person walkthrough videos, interactive 360° panoramas, and VR technology. The project is designed to improve campus accessibility, orientation, and the visitor experience through an engaging digital platform.
